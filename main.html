Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of "extraction," stealing secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully implant an idea into a target's mind, a process known as "inception." As he assembles a team and delves deeper into layers of dreams, the lines between reality and dreams blur, leading to a suspenseful and thought-provoking journey.
